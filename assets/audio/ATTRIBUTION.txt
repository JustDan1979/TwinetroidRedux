Twinetroid Redux Attributions
============================

Original Work
- “Twinetroid” is a Twine adaptation of the Metroid novelization.
- Publisher: Futabasha
- Writer: Noboyuki Shioda
- Illustrator: Arisaka Sumi

Project Origin
- This repository is a quality-of-life (QOL) build of the Twinetroid story hosted at:
  https://metroiddatabase.com/a-merry-twinetroid-christmas

Image Sources
- Base Twinetroid artwork and maps downloaded from Metroid Database:
  https://metroiddatabase.com/wp-content/uploads/Metroid/Twinetroid/ (retrieved 2026-02-20)
- Zeb image: https://metroid.fandom.com/wiki/Zeb (retrieved 2026-02-20)
- Sidehopper image: https://metroid.fandom.com/wiki/Sidehopper (retrieved 2026-02-20)
- Rinka image: https://metroid.fandom.com/wiki/Rinka (retrieved 2026-02-20)
- Multiviola image: https://metroid.fandom.com/wiki/Multiviola (retrieved 2026-02-20)

Notes
- All images are converted to .webp for consistency.
- Zeb/Sidehopper/Rinka/Multiviola are displayed in grayscale in-game to match the rest of the artwork.

Audio
- Procedural audio generated in-game; no external audio files are used.
